We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

ProDown uses third-party APIs (such as Cobalt and TiklyDown) to extract publicly available media. These services process only the video URL you provide. We do not share any personal data with these services beyond what is technically necessary to fulfill your request.
