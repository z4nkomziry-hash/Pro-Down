Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't my video downloading?

The video may be set to private, or the platform may have updated its API. Make sure the video is public, then try again. If the issue persists, let us know via Telegram.

Is ProDown safe to use?

Yes. ProDown runs entirely in your browser. We don't store your video URLs or personal data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Why is there a watermark on my download?

Some platforms embed watermarks directly in the video stream. ProDown attempts to find the watermark-free version first. If unavailable, the platform-watermarked version is provided as a fallback.

Is it legal to download social media videos?

You may only download content you own or have explicit permission to download. Downloading copyrighted content without authorization may violate applicable laws and platform terms of service. See our Terms of Service.
